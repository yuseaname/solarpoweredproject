Cost guide

Solar system cost breakdown

Typical costs for panels, batteries, inverters, and the balance of system. Use this to plan your budget.

Estimates vary by location, size, and brand.

Key takeaways

  • Panels are usually the largest hardware cost.
  • Batteries can double total cost for off-grid systems.
  • Balance-of-system parts add meaningful cost.

Typical cost ranges

Component Typical range Notes
Panels $0.40 - $1.20 per watt Cost drops at higher wattage
Inverter $0.20 - $0.70 per watt Depends on type and features
Batteries $200 - $900 per kWh Li-ion usually higher upfront
Balance of system 10% - 25% of total Wiring, mounts, breakers

What changes the price?

System size, battery choice, inverter type, mounting complexity, and local labor rates all affect totals.
