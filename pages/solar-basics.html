Solar power basics

This guide explains what solar power is, how it works, and when it makes sense for different use cases.

Updated for evergreen clarity. Informational only.

Key takeaways

  • Solar panels convert sunlight into usable electricity.
  • Systems can be grid-tied, hybrid, or off-grid.
  • Costs depend on size, components, and installation.

What solar power is

Solar power uses photovoltaic (PV) panels to turn sunlight into direct current (DC) electricity. An inverter converts that DC into alternating current (AC) for typical appliances.

Ad slot

How solar systems work

A complete system includes panels, an inverter, wiring, and often a battery. The flow is: sunlight → panel output → charge controller (optional) → inverter → appliances.
