Maintenance

Solar maintenance checklist

Routine maintenance keeps output consistent and helps you spot issues early.

For safety, follow manufacturer and local code guidance.

Key takeaways

  • Clean panels as needed, not on a fixed schedule.
  • Inspect wiring, mounts, and inverter alerts.
  • Monitor performance and compare to expected output.

Monthly checklist

  • Check system dashboard for alerts.
  • Look for shading changes or debris.
  • Inspect visible wiring and mounts.
