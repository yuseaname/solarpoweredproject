  - Strong internal linking opportunities

Each phase produces:

════════════════════════════════════
EXECUTION STEPS (STRICT ORDER)
════════════════════════════════════

FOR EACH PHASE, YOU MUST:

STEP 1: NICHE RESEARCH
• Analyze the website’s niche and existing content
• Identify high-RPM informational subtopics
• Prioritize:
  - Cost-related queries
  - Comparisons
  - Pros vs cons
  - “Is it worth it?” intent
  - How-it-works explainers

STEP 2: TOPIC SELECTION
• Choose TWO closely related topics
• Explain briefly WHY these topics were chosen
• Confirm:
  - Search intent alignment
  - AdSense suitability
  - No keyword cannibalization

STEP 3: SEO VALIDATION
• Define:
  - Primary keyword
  - Secondary keywords
  - Search intent

STEP 4: OUTLINE CREATION (FOR EACH ARTICLE)
Each outline must include:
• SEO-optimized H1
• Logical H2/H3 hierarchy
• Clear intent satisfaction
• FAQ section
• Natural internal link placements
• Suggested AdSense placement zones

STEP 5: FULL ARTICLE CREATION
• Long-form, in-depth content
• Written for humans, structured for search engines
• Neutral, educational tone
• Clear explanations
• No fluff
• No filler
• No AI footprints

STEP 6: INTERNAL LINKING LOGIC
• Link article A → article B (if relevant)
• Link both articles:
  - Up to pillar hub
  - Sideways to 2–3 related articles
• Include “Next logical read” suggestions

STEP 7: QUALITY CONTROL
Before finalizing, verify:
• Search intent fully satisfied
• No redundancy
• No thin sections
• High informational value
• AdSense-safe language
