Ground rules (so this stays useful)

DIY is great when you stay honest about losses and limits.

No overunity / “free energy”

If a device claims more energy out than in without an external source (sun, water flow, fuel, wind), it’s not a real power source. This pillar focuses on proven energy sources and realistic conversions.

Safety first

Electricity, spinning shafts, water intakes, and batteries can all injure people or start fires. Use proper protection, follow local codes where applicable, and stop if you’re unsure.
